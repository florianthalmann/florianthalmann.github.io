This page gathers some of my past and current musical projects. I am mostly interested in electronic music, improvised, and experimental music. I perform with computer and electronics, trumpet, Akai EWI, saxophones, and keyboards in various musical formations. I also compose, produce, and record experimental electronic music, jazz, and popular music.
